F1 Race

Port of the game from MTK OS to SDL2 and Emscripten.

Controls:

  • Arrows and 4, 6, 8, 2 on Keypad – Movement.
  • Space, Return and 5 on Keypad – Fly.
  • Tab, N and 0 on Keypad – Switch MIDI.
  • M and 7 on Keypad – Mute.
